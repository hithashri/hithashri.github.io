# Tokyo Olympics 2021 Analytics Study

## Executive Overview

Conducted exploratory data analysis on Tokyo 2021 Olympics datasets to identify medal trends, participation patterns, and contextual factors affecting performance.

## Business Context

Sports analytics provides interpretable insights for strategic planning, representation analysis, and event-level performance benchmarking.

## Problem Statement

What key patterns can be extracted from Olympic data related to medal distribution, gender representation, host nation dynamics, and newly introduced sports?

## Solution Design

- Cleaned and structured multi-table Olympics datasets for analysis.
- Performed trend analysis across countries, sports, and medal categories.
- Evaluated representation and participation shifts by gender and event type.
- Investigated host nation effects and COVID-era contextual impacts.

## System Design and Architecture

- **Data layer:** Ingested and joined Olympics event, athlete, and medal datasets.
- **Analysis layer:** Exploratory statistics, comparative slicing, and trend discovery.
- **Visualization layer:** Insight-focused charts for narrative reporting.
